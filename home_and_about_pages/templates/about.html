So... About Us...

SelfieBook comes to you from the portfolio of AstralWeeks.


Our Mission

Taking a daily selfie as a record of a season, year or even decade has become greatly popular online.

SelfieBook is a small-scaled application designed to show off developer skills. As some people have taken daily photographs every day of their life, as a kind of photographic diary, I thought that an application where people can store these photos would be handy.

Although it is a portfolio project, it is also quite a useful concept. As such, feel free to document your seasons and years here at SelfieBook, and share them with friends by asking them to signup and link to each other by friend requests.

SelfieBook is powered by Django. Check out Django at the Django Project website.
